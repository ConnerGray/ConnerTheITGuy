Top 10 Tech Gifts for the 2021 Holiday Season

December 8th, 2021

Introduction

In this blog post I will be giving my top 10 picks for the best tech gifts for the holiday season. I decided not to put a price cap on my picks, but most gifts will be around $100 with a few being a little higher priced. These gifts will be great for friends and family this holiday season. Now let’s get into it!



1. Elgato Stream Deck Mini - $80

The Stream Deck Mini is a great gift for not only gamers but anyone who uses a computer daily. The mini comes with 6 buttons that can be programmed to do just about anything. If you need more, you can purchase a 15-button and a 32-button model.


2. Wyze Cam v3 - $35

If you are looking for a cheap home camera the Wyze cam is the way to go! For only $35 you get a small form factor wired 1080p HD camera with 2-way audio and night vision. The Wyze cam is also compatible with Google assistant and Alexa.


3. AirPods Gen 3 - $179

In my opinion everyone could use a pair of wireless earphones and the AirPods rule the market! Apple’s latest model of the AirPods released this past fall and have a longer battery life and improved sound quality.


4. Oculus Quest 2 - $300

The Oculus is a great gift for anyone looking to try out VR. VR is an interesting experience that every should have and the way things are going VR is the future.


5. 4K Fire Stick TV - $30

A Fire Stick is always a go to gift every holiday season. For only $30 you get a 4K capable device that brings everything in the world of media straight to your television.


6. Portable MagSafe Charger - $60

A portable MagSafe charger is a great gift for anyone with a iPhone 12 and newer model iPhone. I can’t recall how many times I have been on the go with my phone running out of juice!


7. AirTag - $30

The AirTag is another great gift for anyone you know in the Apple eco system. The AirTag is a small circle tracking device. You can buy a small holder for the AirTag and attach it to your keys, backpack, bike, wallet, etc.


8. Case-Mate Power Pad 3 in 1 - $129

The Case Mate power pad is another great gift to clean up your nightstand and takes care of all your chagrining needs!


9. Google Nest Hub - $100

The Google Nest Hub is one of my favorites on this list! I got my mom one last Christmas, and she absolutely loves it! The Nest Hub comes with Google Assistant that can do plenty of cool things! You can also display a slideshow of all your pictures in Google Photos!


10. Apple 9th-Gen iPad - $329

Last on our list is the latest lower model Apple iPad. An iPad can be a great gift for anyone from kids to elders. Anyone can get a lot of use and have plenty of things to from content consumption, gaming, web browsing & more!
