First Blog Ever!

December 28th 2020



For those of you who don't know me my name is Conner. I am a system administrator located in Dayton, Ohio. I have been in the IT industry for 3 years now. Computers and technology have been a passion of mine since I can remember.

I went to a career center my last two years of high school to study computer programming with an emphasis in web programming. After graduating from high school I continued on at my local community college and received my associates degree in Secure Systems Administration. My last year of college I landed an internship with an aerospace company in the Dayton area. After interning for a year I was hired full time as a systems administrator and have been working there ever since! 

Outside of my work I have many other interest including sports, videography, and playing with my pup! I also run a podcast with my brother and two of our friends called Cavemen Media! you can check that out at Cavemen-media.com.
