4 Ways to Utilize ChatGPT in Everyday Life

Published on 6/16/2023

What is ChatGPT?

Unless you have been living under a rock you have probably heard of ChatGPT by now. ChatGPT is a chat bot powered by GPT which gives AI the ability to generate text. ChatGPT is essentially and AI app that was created by the company OpenAI. ChatGPT-4 is the newest version of the app and has some more advanced capabilities such as creativity, visual input, and longer context. In this blog post we will be going over 4 ways you can use ChatGPT in your everyday life.

Create meal ideas and shopping lists

One way you can utilize ChatGPT is by asking it to create a list of meal ideas for you. You could ask it to create a weeks’ worth of lunch or dinner recopies. In addition to getting a list of meal ideas for the week ChatGPT will also give you a complete shopping list of everything you will need. You can also tell ChatGPT thing you do and don’t like, and it will take that into consideration when creating recipes! This can be a quick way to get meals planned for the entire week for the whole family!

Summarize books and articles

Another great way to use ChatGPT is to ask it to summarize and entire book or article for you. This is one that I use often. To accomplish this, navigate to the article you want to summarize. Copy the contents of the article and paste that into ChatGPT and it will provide you with a summary. To geta summary of a book you can type in the following prompt: “TLDR: Book title.”

Get recommendations

The third option for utilizing ChatGPT in everyday life is to ask for recommendations. You could use this in several ways. You can ask for recommendations on Netflix shows or movies, books to read, which car to buy, the best schools in your area, etc. There are endless possibilities.

Learn something new

The last way to utilize ChatGPT is by learning something new. You can ask ChatGPT to teach you about any topic and it will provide you with information on the topic. For example, you could ask “Teach me how to unclog a bathtub drain.” It will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to unclog a drain. You can learn other things too such as a new language, how to cook, and how to lose weight!

Conclusion

In conclusion, ChatGPT is an amazing AI tool that can be utilized by anyone. There are hundreds of different prompts you can ask ChatGPT. This is just one example of AI tools that are available today. To get started playing around with ChatGPT head on over to their website and create a free account!
